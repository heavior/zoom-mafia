Quick Rules
[x]
You randomly join a team: mafia or civilians, and your objective is to eliminate opponents
Game has 4 phases:
  1. Day - discussion: discuss your suspicions, build alliances
  2. Day - main vote: choose who is guilty
  3. Day - last word: convicted people has their last chance to sway public opinion
  4. Night: civilians sleep, mafia kills a player, detective check the role of one player
    1. Players murdered at night do not have a last word
Hints for civilians:
  • Start the first day by introducing yourself and convincing other players that you are civilian
  • Be active, voice your thoughts and suspicions, that helps your team
  • Watch how people act, what do the say and what do they do
Hints for mafia:
  • Build alliances with civilians during daytime
  • Pay attention to your night vote, do not miss
Hints for detective:
  • You are playing for civilians
  • Hide your identity from mafia
Fair game rules:
  • Do not show your screen or give out any other evidence of your role 
  • Do not take oaths during the game
  • Any verbal claim for role can be a deception
  • Dead players and guests do not participate the discussion or affect the game
  • Do not text, use only voice and video
