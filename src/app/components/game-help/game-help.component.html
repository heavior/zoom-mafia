Quick Rules [ << hide ]
You randomly join a team: mafia or civilians, your objective is to eliminate opponents
Game has 4 phases:
  1. Day - discussion: discuss your suspicions, build alliances. Start the game by introducing yourself and convincing that you are innocent
  2. Day - main vote: choose who is guilty
  3. Day - last word: convicted people have their last chance to sway public opinion
  4. Night: civilians sleep, mafia kills a player, detective checks the role of one player. Players murdered at night do not have a last word
  • All phases except discussion have limited time
Vote rules:
  • Each vote is open
  • Main vote is mandatory, abstained votes count for the first candidate
  • If there is a tie during daytime - players vote to save or convict all candidates
  • If there is a tie during nighttime - mafia misses
Fair game rules:
  • Do not show your screen or give out any other evidence of your role 
  • Do not take oaths during the game
  • Anyone who claims a role could be lying
  • Dead players and guests do not participate the discussion or affect the game
  • Do not text, use only voice and video
Game moderator:
  • Moderator also has a role and plays for some team
  • If moderator is not alive in the game - he still acts as moderator
  • Moderator’s responsibilities:
    • start the game when players are ready
    • facilitate the discussion
    • start main vote, and start the night
    • restart the game if players agree to
    • remove players who lost internet and cannot reconnect
