What is it?
Mafia is a party game that used to require a room, a game master and a deck of cards. We made it very easy to play remotely over the video conference:
  1. Create a room
  2. Share a link in a video chat
  3. Wait for your friends to open the link
  4. Enjoy
Why?
Mafia is our favorite social game. This sophisticated game used to be played in bars, in dormitories, on camping, and even in professional setting in clubs.

Now with everyone staying at home, when everything is closed, we all need more ways to stay connected and stay sane. This game is our contribution to the common cause.
